Studio FAQ & Policies

Everything you need to know about deposits, grace windows, companion limits, and natural nail protection rules.

The private studio is located at Dipo Omotayo, Off Addo Road, Ajah Lekki, Lagos. It's a quiet, intimate space designed for an unhurried appointment experience. To maintain client privacy, the exact building number and directions are sent directly after your deposit is confirmed.

Since Niru only takes 5–6 clients daily, each slot is a dedicated block of undivided attention. A deposit is required to hold your slot, confirmed directly on WhatsApp after booking. It is non-refundable if you cancel with less than 24 hours notice or fail to show.

To guarantee absolute focus, we operate on an appointment-only basis. Walk-ins are not accommodated to ensure our current guests enjoy a peaceful, quiet, soft-girl experience without interruptions.

I take my time with prep and use quality products, so my sets typically last 4 to 6 weeks without lifting. Just keep your cuticles oiled and you're good.

Arriving more than 15 minutes late incurs a ₦5,000 fee. Arriving more than 30 minutes late incurs a ₦10,000 fee, and your appointment may need to be rescheduled.

If you don't show up without prior notice, your deposit is non-refundable and can't be moved to a future booking.

The studio is designed as an intimate, relaxing escape with limited seating space. In order to keep your appointment serene and focused, we hold a strict "no extra guests, companions, or children" policy.
